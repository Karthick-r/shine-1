Shine Threading

Eyebrows are the character of face and yet underestimated by many. They frame the face and draw the attention to your eyes. What could be a better tool than threading to achieve those perfectly defined eyebrows. Threading is a hair removal technique using cotton thread. It has been commonly practised in eastern countries, but is becoming fast popular in west and is even endorsed by celebrities. Threading gives the brows a defined look, as it can remove the finest hairs and skilfully deliver the shape that other hair removal techniques fail to deliver.

Shine Waxing

Waxing is a form of semi-permanent body and facial hair removal which removes the hair from the root. New hair will not grow back in the previously waxed area for two to six weeks, although this may vary from client to client. Almost any area of the body can be waxed, including eyebrows, face, legs, arms, back, feet and intimate area. Shine offers both hot and strip waxing to cater to the varied client requirements.

Shine Nail Extension

Let your hands do the talking with beautiful acrylic nail extensions from Shine Beauty Lounge. Acrylics are the oldest form of nail extension in the modern beauty industry, and the reason they have remained popular is due to their staying power.  The acrylic itself is a mixture of liquid monomer and a powder polymer, which when combined, form a hard and protective layer that is sculpted over the natural nail.

Acrylic extensions will only become hard once they have been exposed to air. When this happens, and the process is complete, the nails become a strong and glossy base that is perfect for applying your chosen colour.

The procedure for acrylic extensions is a multi-step process. First, your nail technician will prepare the natural nail by cleaning, shaping, buffing and working the cuticle. Then the artificial tip is applied to add length and finally the acrylic is applied on top. Once this has been cured, it can then be painted.

The life of acrylic nail extensions will vary from person to person and is dependent on a number of different factors. We recommend that you have your acrylics either touched up with infills, or completely removed every three to four weeks.

Important: Acrylic nail extensions must be removed professionally to avoid damaging the nail. Pop back in and we’ll remove them with the safe and gently respected remover Wraps by experienced nail technician.
